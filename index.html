Who We are

BidUp is an auction system for sellers and bidders based in Rajkot, Gujarat, India. Our platform provides a seamless and secure experience for both parties.

With BidUp, sellers can easily list their items and reach a wider audience, while bidders can bid on items they want and track their progress in real-time. Trust and transparency are at the core of our business, ensuring a fair and efficient auction process for all.
